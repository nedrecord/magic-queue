Placard Generator

This tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you upload is sent to the server.

Inputs

Summon QR ZIP

Upload the ZIP of summon QR codes you downloaded from the dashboard (magic-queue-qrs.zip).

Tip QR (optional)

Upload your Venmo/Zelle QR image if you want tipping printed on the cards. Leave this blank if you don't want a tip QR on the placards.

Each placard will automatically include the summon QR for that table. If you provide a tip QR, it will be placed on the right with your tip line.
